# YarnZoo Brand Styling

## Overview

Use this skill to apply **YarnZoo’s official brand identity** to content, visuals, or generated artifacts.  
The style is friendly, creative, approachable, and recognisably YarnZoo — suitable for crochet patterns, guides, marketing materials, and community content.

**Keywords**: branding, visual identity, YarnZoo, crochet brand, amigurumi, post-processing, styling, typography, brand colors

---

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors

**Primary Colors**

- **Primary Accent**: `#E74016`  
  Used for headings, links, buttons, and key highlights.
- **Soft Accent Background**: `#FFEDEC`  
  Used for subtle section backgrounds, callouts, and panels.
- **Base Background**: `#FFFFFF`  
  Default background color.
- **Primary Text**: `#444249`  
  Body text and long-form readability.

**Usage principles**

- Orange is expressive but never overpowering.
- Light pink is used sparingly to soften layouts.
- White space is essential to keep patterns and instructions readable.

---

### Typography

- **Primary Font (Headings & Body)**: Campton Medium  
- **Fallback**: sans-serif

YarnZoo uses **one consistent typeface** across headings and body text to maintain clarity and a friendly, recognisable rhythm.

---

## Styling Features

### Unified Font Application

- Headings and body text use **Campton Medium**
- Falls back to `sans-serif` if unavailable
- No decorative or script fonts
- Optimized for instructional and pattern-based content

---

### Text Styling Rules

- Headings and body share the same font family
- Hierarchy is created through spacing, weight, and color
- Headings typically use YarnZoo Orange (`#E74016`)
- Body text remains neutral for long reading sessions

---

### Shapes, Accents & UI Elements

- Buttons, icons, and highlights use `#E74016`
- Subtle containers may use `#FFEDEC`
- Borders are soft and neutral (`#E6E6E6`)
- Rounded corners: **6px**
- Shadows are avoided unless functionally necessary

---

## Component Styling

### Inputs & Form Elements

- Background: transparent or white
- Text color: `#444249`
- Border: `#E6E6E6`
- Border radius: `6px`
- No heavy shadows

Designed to feel calm, friendly, and non-technical.

---

## Technical Notes

### Font Management

- Uses system-installed **Campton Medium** where available
- Falls back to standard `sans-serif`
- No font installation required
- Suitable for web, PDF, and pattern exports

---

### Color Application

- All colors defined as HEX values
- Suitable for programmatic styling
- High contrast maintained for accessibility and print clarity

---

## Brand Intent

YarnZoo styling should always feel:

- Friendly, never corporate  
- Creative, but easy to read  
- Calm and trustworthy  
- Suitable for beginners and experienced crocheters  

This skill prioritizes **readability, warmth, and consistency** over visual noise.
